---
title: 'Connecting Github'
---

In order to deploy apps in private repositories, you must connect your Github so as to provide authorization. The steps are as follows:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go To Settings">
    ![Connect Github](/images/connect_github.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize Deploy To Access Your Private Repos">
    ![Authorize Deploy](/images/github_auth.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="You will see that your Github account is connected">
    ![Github Connected](/images/github_connected.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>


<Tip>To disconnect simply tap the `Disconnect Github` button and your Github will be unlinked</Tip>
